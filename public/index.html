Faisal Mosque, Islamabad

The Faisal Mosque in Islamabad is one of the largest mosques in the world and an iconic symbol of Pakistan. Nestled at the foot of the Margalla Hills, its unique tent-shaped design makes it a must-visit landmark for every traveler.

Share a trip

Daman-e-Koh, Islamabad

Daman-e-Koh is a hilltop garden and viewpoint located in the Margalla Hills above Islamabad. It offers a breathtaking panoramic view of the entire city and is a popular spot for nature lovers and tourists alike.

Share a trip

Khanpur Dam, KPK

Khanpur Dam is a stunning reservoir located near Islamabad on the Haro River. Famous for its crystal-clear turquoise water, it is a top destination for boating, jet skiing, and weekend getaways from the twin cities.
